SIM AH BENIN

Description

  • PRIVEE

SIM AH is a solution for farmers that addresses the following different points:
- Marketing facilitation
Allows producers to know the best time to sell their products and at what price thanks to information relating to prices charged and supply and demand.
- Information provided in real time
Users who are producers, resellers, transporters or even end consumers regularly receive information on prices in the markets through calls and SMS.
- Market transparency and fairness
By providing price information, the solution contributes to greater transparency and fairness in the market
- Time saving
SIM AH eliminates the need to visit multiple markets to compare prices, saving users time
- Support for farmers
The app helps direct wholesalers and transporters to economically advantageous markets, benefiting local farmers and the local economy
- Valuable data for research and decision-making
Data collected by the app is used for research into food price trends and to inform government policies aimed at improving food security
