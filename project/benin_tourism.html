BENIN TOURISM

Description

  • www.benintourisme.net

It is a site that allows hotel owners to put their establishments online in order to find new customers. Customers can also consult the detailed presentation of the hotels and their rates in real time and make a reservation online without having to travel. It also offers the possibility to leave comments on the hotels, to send invoices directly by email, and many other features.
