MAYDAY PHONE

Description

  • www.maydayphone.com/

This platform contains several modules:
- An ecommerce module for the sale of electronic devices
- An online quotation system for the repair of several brands of telephones, with appointment booking.
- A system for estimating the price of phones for sale for reconditioning
