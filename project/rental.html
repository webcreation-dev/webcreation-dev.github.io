Rental Management

Description

  • github.rental_management.com

It is a platform for the management of a company specialized in renting cars, apartments, audio-visual materials and materials, audio visuals, and many other products. More specifically, its customer management management, its rental management, its inventory management, its revenue management and collections management. It offers the possibility to have a visibility of of the activity related to the cash desk (cash in/out) and to be notified of of each operation carried out, an e-mail at the end of the day and a report on the rentals made (classified by product), the revenue made and what remains to be remaining to be collected.
