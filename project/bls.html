BANK LEGAL SOFT

Description

  • PRIVEE

Bank Legal Soft is a state-of-the-art software designed for banks to provide top-notch legal and company secretariat services with a focus on efficiency and effectiveness. Leveraging user-friendly paperless technology, it ensures cost-cutting and streamlined operations. Compliant with laws and regulations in UMOA and CEMAC countries, this multilingual solution (available in English, French, etc.) supports desktop, laptop, iPad, and smartphone platforms. Bank Legal Soft enhances subsidiary governance by improving oversight and communication between the Group and its subsidiaries. It facilitates efficient information flow, decision management, and execution through meticulous planning and control mechanisms. Key features include automated management of legal processes, data, deadlines, and milestones, along with effective task monitoring and performance control. Specific functions encompass corporate event planning, board composition compliance, director and CEO mandate management, securities perfection, and contract monitoring to ensure compliance and prevent defaults.
